Rhode Island Works (TANF)

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Rhode Island Works is the states Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF) program. It provides temporary monthly cash assistance and employment support to eligible low-income families with children. Payment amounts vary based on family size, income, and circumstances.

Who is eligible

Rhode Island Works is generally for families with dependent children who meet income and resource limits. Applicants typically must be Rhode Island residents and may participate in employment or training activities. The Rhode Island Department of Human Services reviews each application to determine eligibility.

What you can get

The program provides temporary monthly cash payments along with employment services and supports. The cash amount varies by family size and income and is intended as short-term help while families work toward stability.

How to apply

You can apply for Rhode Island Works online, by mail, or in person through the Rhode Island Department of Human Services. Applicants provide information about household, income, and children, and may complete an interview.

Official source: Rhode Island Department of Human Services RI Works
