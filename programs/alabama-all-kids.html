Alabama ALL Kids (CHIP)

Last updated: June 14, 2026

ALL Kids is Alabama's Children's Health Insurance Program (CHIP) coverage. It offers low-cost, comprehensive health coverage for eligible children in working families whose income is too high for Medicaid but who still need affordable coverage. It is administered by the Alabama Department of Public Health (ADPH).

What is ALL Kids?

ALL Kids provides comprehensive health coverage for eligible children, including doctor visits, immunizations, prescriptions, dental, vision, and hospital care. Costs are low-cost depending on family income.

Who is eligible?

Children in families that meet the income guidelines may qualify. Eligibility is based on the child's age, family size, and household income. ADPH reviews each application against current CHIP rules.

How to apply

You can apply for ALL Kids online, by mail, or by phone through the Alabama Department of Public Health. The program will review your application and may request documents to verify income.
