South Carolina Healthy Connections (Medicaid)

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Healthy Connections is South Carolina's Medicaid program. It provides free or low-cost health coverage to eligible residents, including families, children, pregnant women, older adults, and people with disabilities. It is administered by the South Carolina Department of Health and Human Services (SCDHHS).

What is Healthy Connections?

Healthy Connections Medicaid covers a wide range of health services such as doctor visits, hospital care, prescriptions, and preventive care. Covered services and any small costs vary depending on the specific coverage category you qualify for.

Who is eligible?

Eligibility depends on income, household size, age, and other factors. Children, pregnant women, parents, older adults, and people with disabilities who meet income limits may qualify. SCDHHS reviews each application against current rules.

How to apply

You can apply for Healthy Connections online, by phone, by mail, or in person. After applying, SCDHHS reviews your information and may request documents to verify income and household details.
