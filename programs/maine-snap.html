Maine SNAP / Food Supplement Program

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Maine calls its Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program the Food Supplement Program. It helps eligible low-income households in Maine afford groceries each month through an EBT card. The program is administered by the Maine Department of Health and Human Services, Office for Family Independence.

What is the Food Supplement Program?

The Food Supplement Program is Maine name for SNAP, the federal nutrition assistance program. Eligible households receive monthly benefits loaded onto an EBT card that can be used to buy food at participating grocery stores and farmers markets. Benefit amounts vary based on household size, income, and certain expenses.

Who may be eligible?

Eligibility depends on household size, income, and resources. Maine reviews gross and net monthly income against federal limits, and certain deductions such as housing and child care costs are considered. Many working families, older adults, and people with disabilities qualify. The exact benefit amount varies by household circumstances.

How to apply in Maine

You can apply online through My Maine Connection, by mail, or in person at a local DHHS office. After applying, most households complete an interview and provide documents to verify income and expenses. The Office for Family Independence processes applications and determines eligibility.

Official source: Maine DHHS - Food Supplement (SNAP)

Frequently asked questions

How much will I get in Food Supplement benefits?

The amount varies based on your household size, income, and allowable deductions. Maine determines your monthly benefit after reviewing your application and supporting documents.

Where can I use my Maine EBT card?

You can use your EBT card at most grocery stores, many convenience stores, and participating farmers markets across Maine to buy eligible food items.

How do I apply for Maine SNAP?

Apply online at My Maine Connection, by mail, or in person at a DHHS office. The Office for Family Independence handles applications and eligibility.
