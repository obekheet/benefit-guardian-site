Alabama Medicaid

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Alabama Medicaid provides free or low-cost health coverage to eligible Alabama residents, including children, pregnant women, older adults, and people with disabilities. It is administered by the Alabama Medicaid Agency.

What is Alabama Medicaid?

Alabama Medicaid covers a wide range of health services such as doctor visits, hospital care, prescriptions, and preventive care. Covered services and any small costs vary depending on the coverage category you qualify for.

Who is eligible?

Eligibility depends on income, household size, age, and other factors. Children, pregnant women, older adults, and people with disabilities who meet income limits may qualify. The Alabama Medicaid Agency reviews each application against current rules.

How to apply

You can apply for Alabama Medicaid online, by mail, or in person. After applying, the agency reviews your information and may request documents to verify income and household details.
