Frequently asked questions

What benefit programs are available in Connecticut?

Connecticut residents may qualify for several programs, including Connecticut HUSKY B (Children's Health Insurance Program), Connecticut HUSKY Health (Medicaid), Connecticut SNAP (Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program), Connecticut Temporary Family Assistance (TFA / TANF). Eligibility depends on household size, income, and other factors.

How do I know if I qualify for benefits in Connecticut?

Each Connecticut program has its own income and eligibility rules. Benefit Guardian lets you check your likely eligibility across all of these programs in a few minutes.

Can I receive more than one Connecticut benefit at the same time?

Yes. Many households qualify for more than one program at once — for example, both Medicaid and SNAP. You can apply to each program you may be eligible for.

Are these Connecticut benefit programs free to apply for?

Yes. Applying for state and federal benefit programs in Connecticut is always free through the official state agencies. Benefit Guardian never charges to check your eligibility.
