Frequently asked questions

What benefit programs are available in South Carolina?

South Carolina residents may qualify for several programs, including South Carolina Family Independence (TANF), South Carolina Healthy Connections (Medicaid), South Carolina Partners for Healthy Children (CHIP), South Carolina SNAP / Food Assistance. Eligibility depends on household size, income, and other factors.

How do I know if I qualify for benefits in South Carolina?

Each South Carolina program has its own income and eligibility rules. Benefit Guardian lets you check your likely eligibility across all of these programs in a few minutes.

Can I receive more than one South Carolina benefit at the same time?

Yes. Many households qualify for more than one program at once — for example, both Medicaid and SNAP. You can apply to each program you may be eligible for.

Are these South Carolina benefit programs free to apply for?

Yes. Applying for state and federal benefit programs in South Carolina is always free through the official state agencies. Benefit Guardian never charges to check your eligibility.
