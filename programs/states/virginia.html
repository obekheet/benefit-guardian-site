Frequently asked questions

What benefit programs are available in Virginia?

Virginia residents may qualify for several programs, including Virginia Energy Assistance Program (EAP), virginia-medicaid.html, virginia-snap.html, Virginia TANF (Cash Assistance). Eligibility depends on household size, income, and other factors.

How do I know if I qualify for benefits in Virginia?

Each Virginia program has its own income and eligibility rules. Benefit Guardian lets you check your likely eligibility across all of these programs in a few minutes.

Can I receive more than one Virginia benefit at the same time?

Yes. Many households qualify for more than one program at once — for example, both Medicaid and SNAP. You can apply to each program you may be eligible for.

Are these Virginia benefit programs free to apply for?

Yes. Applying for state and federal benefit programs in Virginia is always free through the official state agencies. Benefit Guardian never charges to check your eligibility.
