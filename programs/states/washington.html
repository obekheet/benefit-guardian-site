Frequently asked questions

What benefit programs are available in Washington?

Washington residents may qualify for several programs, including Washington Aged, Blind or Disabled (ABD) Cash Program, Washington Apple Health (Medicaid), washington-snap.html, Washington TANF (Cash Assistance). Eligibility depends on household size, income, and other factors.

How do I know if I qualify for benefits in Washington?

Each Washington program has its own income and eligibility rules. Benefit Guardian lets you check your likely eligibility across all of these programs in a few minutes.

Can I receive more than one Washington benefit at the same time?

Yes. Many households qualify for more than one program at once — for example, both Medicaid and SNAP. You can apply to each program you may be eligible for.

Are these Washington benefit programs free to apply for?

Yes. Applying for state and federal benefit programs in Washington is always free through the official state agencies. Benefit Guardian never charges to check your eligibility.
