Texas SNAP (Lone Star Card) Food Benefits

Last updated: June 13, 2026

The Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) in Texas helps people with low income buy food. Benefits are delivered each month on the Lone Star Card, which works like a debit card at grocery stores and many other retailers. This guide explains who is eligible and how to apply through Texas Health and Human Services.

Who is eligible?

Eligibility for SNAP in Texas is based on your household size, income, and certain expenses. Most households must meet income limits, and some applicants (such as those who are working, elderly, or have a disability) may have different rules. Resources and citizenship/immigration status can also affect eligibility. Because the income limits change periodically, confirm the current thresholds for your household size at the official Texas Health and Human Services source.

How much can you get?

The amount depends on household size, income, and expenses. Texas Health and Human Services publishes a chart of the maximum monthly benefit by family size: for a family of four, the maximum is $994 per month. Smaller and larger households receive different amounts, and for each additional person beyond eight the maximum increases. The figure shown is the maximum — your actual benefit may be lower depending on your income.

How to apply and official source

You can apply for SNAP in Texas online through YourTexasBenefits, by phone, or in person. This information is based on Texas Health and Human Services: hhs.texas.gov — SNAP Food Benefits. Verify current eligibility, benefit amounts, and how to apply at the official source.

Frequently asked questions

What is the Lone Star Card?

The Lone Star Card is the card Texas uses to deliver SNAP food benefits each month. It works like a debit card at grocery stores and other approved retailers.

How much is Texas SNAP for a family of four?

Texas Health and Human Services lists a maximum monthly benefit of $994 for a family of four. Your actual amount depends on your household's income and expenses.

How do I apply for SNAP in Texas?

You can apply online through YourTexasBenefits, by phone, or in person. See hhs.texas.gov for current application options and eligibility details.

What can I buy with SNAP benefits?

SNAP benefits can be used to buy food items such as fruits, vegetables, meat, dairy, bread, and other groceries. They cannot be used for alcohol, tobacco, or non-food items.
