Try It Free

Most people have no idea what their credit cards, subscriptions, and government programs offer them. Benefit Guardian is a tool that helps you cut through that complexity — so you can see what you may already qualify for and decide what's worth claiming.

What we do

  • ✅ We never ask for your credit card numbers — only the names of cards you have.
  • ✅ We check public information about card perks, membership credits, and government programs.
  • ✅ We surface programs that may apply to your situation, with steps for you to verify and apply yourself.
  • ✅ We do not take any action on your behalf — you stay in control.

Who it's for

Anyone with a credit card, a paid subscription, or a household budget. Whether you have one card or ten, we help you spot benefits you may have missed.

What we are not

Benefit Guardian is not a financial advisor, legal advisor, or government agency. We don't promise specific savings amounts. We don't guarantee eligibility for any program — only the issuing agency or company can determine that.
